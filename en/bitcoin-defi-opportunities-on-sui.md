## BTCfi on Sui: A Better Way to Use Bitcoin

Explore the growing BTCfi ecosystem on Sui and how it unlocks real DeFi utility for Bitcoin.

* [![Sui Foundation](https://blog.sui.io/content/images/size/w100/2023/04/Sui_Droplet_Logo_Blue-3.png)](https://blog.sui.io/author/sui-foundation/)

![BTCfi on Sui: A Better Way to Use Bitcoin](https://blog.sui.io/content/images/size/w2000/2025/07/07-28-Blog-Header.jpg)

Bitcoin has long been known as a passive store of value, but [on Sui, it’s becoming something more](https://blog.sui.io/reimagining-bitcoins-role-in-sui-defi/). Through a [growing set of BTC-backed assets](https://blog.sui.io/bitcoin-backed-assets-powering-btcfi/) and composable protocols, users can now move BTC throughout Sui’s ecosystem and put it to work.

This involvement of BTC-backed assets into DeFi, referred to as [BTCfi](https://sui.io/btcfi-bitcoin-defi-on-sui?ref=blog.sui.io), turns Bitcoin into a liquid, programmable asset on Sui able to push the boundaries of Bitcoin utility. With native support from [DeepBook](https://deepbook.tech/?ref=blog.sui.io) and a wide array of protocols already integrating BTC-backed assets, Sui is quickly becoming one of the most versatile environments for BTCfi in Web3.

## What can you do with BTC on Sui?

Rather than sitting idle, BTC on Sui can be traded, staked, lent, leveraged, or used in complex strategies all through composable, high-performance protocols. Here’s a list of current protocols within Sui’s BTCfi ecosystem and the supported BTC-backed assets for each:

### Trade and LP

Sui’s DeFi ecosystem has several options for those looking to trade and provide liquidity. Leveraging DeepBook as a DeFi primitive, BTC-backed assets on Sui can be traded or pooled fast, cheap, and with minimal slippage. 

* [**7k**](https://7k.ag/swap?ref=blog.sui.io) – DEX aggregator supporting limit orders and dollar cost averaging (DCA) strategies
  * wBTC, LBTC, tBTC, WBTC, xBTC
* [**Aftermath**](https://aftermath.finance/?ref=blog.sui.io) – Leverages multi-asset [Constant Function Market Maker (CFMM)](https://docs.aftermath.finance/pools/constant-function-market-maker?ref=blog.sui.io) pools, providing features such as DCA strategies and limit orders along with LP opportunities
  * wBTC, LBTC, tBTC, WBTC, xBTC
* [**Cetus**](https://www.cetus.zone/?ref=blog.sui.io) – DEX platform offering limit orders, DCA strategies, and LP opportunities
  * wBTC, LBTC, stBTC, WBTC, xBTC
* [**FlowX**](https://flowx.finance/?ref=blog.sui.io) – DEX Aggregator supporting limit orders, time-weighted average price (TWAP) execution strategies
  * wBTC, LBTC, stBTC, tBTC, WBTC, xBTC
* [**Magma**](https://magmafinance.io/?ref=blog.sui.io) – DEX platform and aggregator developing a unique [Adaptive Liquidity Market Maker (ALMM)](https://x.com/Magma_Finance/status/1938251212710912392?ref=blog.sui.io) pool model
  * wBTC, LBTC, stBTC, WBTC
* [**Momentum**](https://app.mmt.finance/?ref=blog.sui.io) – DEX platform with LP opportunities leveraging [CLMM pools](https://docs.mmt.finance/core-mechanics/clmm?ref=blog.sui.io)
  * wBTC, LBTC, stBTC, WBTC, xBTC
* [**Steamm**](https://steamm.fi/?ref=blog.sui.io) – [Superfluid AMM](https://docs.suilend.fi/steamm/what-is-steamm?ref=blog.sui.io) utilizing three types of pricing curves with a direct integration to [SuiLend](https://suilend.fi/?ref=blog.sui.io)
  * wBTC, LBTC, WBTC, xBTC
* [**Turbos**](https://turbos.finance/?ref=blog.sui.io) – [CLMM-based](https://turbos.gitbook.io/turbos/products/concentrated-liquidity-amm?ref=blog.sui.io) DEX supporting limit orders along with providing LP opportunities
  * wBTC, LBTC, stBTC, WBTC, xBTC

BTC-backed assets can be utilized in more advanced financial instruments, including perpetuals and structured products.

## Powered by DeepBook

A core piece of Sui’s DeFi stack, enabling more powerful BTCfi [DeepBook](https://deepbook.tech/?ref=blog.sui.io), Sui’s native onchain central-limit order book. It provides shared liquidity infrastructure for all protocols, making BTC-backed assets highly tradable with strong price discovery and minimal fragmentation. This shared layer is what allows multiple protocols to build seamlessly around the same assets, ensuring liquidity isn’t trapped in silos and that innovative protocols aren’t struggling to bootstrap liquidity.
